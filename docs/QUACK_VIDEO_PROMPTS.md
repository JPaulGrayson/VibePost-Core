# Quack Launch Campaign - Video & Image Prompts for Gemini

## Overview
These prompts are designed to create assets for the "Quack Launch" mystery marketing campaign. The campaign uses curious "Quack?" messages to drive engagement and direct users to ask Grok what it means.

## VIDEO ANIMATIONS

### Prompt 3: Solo Mallard "Quack?" Video (Phase 1 - Single Tweet)
```
A male mallard duck centered on screen, ultra realistic 3D render. Green iridescent head, yellow bill, brown chest, gray body, blue speculum wing patch, orange webbed feet. 

Animation: The duck stands still, then tilts its head to the side curiously. It opens its bill and a speech bubble appears with "Quack?" - curious, questioning tone. The duck holds the pose for a beat, looking directly at the viewer with a knowing expression, as if sharing a secret.

Background: Solid dark gray (#3A3A45), no gradients, no shadows, no floor.
Duration: 3-5 second loop.
Style: Playful, mysterious, intriguing.
```

### Prompt 4: Two Ducks "Quack? / Quack Quack!" Interaction Video (Phase 2)
```
Two ducks facing each other in conversation. On the left, a male mallard duck (green iridescent head, yellow bill, brown chest). On the right, a male wood duck (green-purple crested head, red eye, white chin strap, spotted chestnut chest).

Animation sequence:
1. Both ducks stand facing each other
2. The mallard tilts its head quizzically and opens its bill - speech bubble appears: "Quack?"
3. Brief pause as wood duck considers
4. Wood duck responds enthusiastically, nodding - speech bubble appears: "Quack Quack!"
5. Both ducks look at the viewer knowingly

Background: Solid dark gray (#3A3A45), no gradients, no shadows, no floor.
Duration: 5-7 second loop.
Style: Playful, curious interaction suggesting a secret being shared between insiders.
```

## Campaign Context

**Phase 1:** Quote tweet trending agent/swarm posts with just "Quack?" + solo mallard video
- Creates mystery and curiosity
- Users will ask Grok "What does Quack mean?"
- Drives organic discovery of Quack messaging platform

**Phase 2:** Follow-up with "Quack Quack!" + two duck interaction video
- Rewards curious users who engaged with Phase 1
- Suggests insider knowledge and community
- Links to wizardofquack.com for full reveal

---

## Technical Notes

- Use PNG with alpha channel for static images
- Video should be MP4/MOV compatible with Twitter upload
- Keep file sizes optimized for social media (< 15MB for video)
- Background color #3A3A45 matches VibePost dark theme
